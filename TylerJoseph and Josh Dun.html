About the Band

Twenty One Pilots is a musical duo, they are a pop musical band that has different songs for your different feelings, Tyler Joseph the singer and Josh Dun the Drummer are the bestest of friends. They have produced numerous songs that have hit the top 1s in the charts as well as recently created the longest running live stream.
